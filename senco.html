Website Design & User Experience

SENCO.com was a complete renovation of the old website. As Project Manager, I was responsible for managing a creative team and leading the project from start to finish. We added many features that the previous website lacked, making it more interactive and user-friendly.

With the redesign, SENCO.com received impressive results:
